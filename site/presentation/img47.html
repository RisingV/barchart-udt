We have demonstrated UDT and applications using UDT in many conferences and exhibitions. We have participated in the bandwidth challenge in the annual super computing conference. UDT has helped us to win a bandwidth challenge award in each of the last three years. This year next month we will head to Seattle to demonstrate real time high performance mining of streaming data. We will use UDT to transfer large volumetric datasets from Japan, Korea, Europe, and several locations in the US to the SC05 exhibition floor, and the data will be analyzed there.
