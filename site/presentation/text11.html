Objective & Non-objective

  • Objective

    • For distributed data intensive applications in high speed networks
    • A small number of flows share the abundant bandwidth
    • Efficient, fair, and friendly
    • Configurable
    • Easily deployable and usable

  • Non-objective

    • Replace TCP on the Internet

Notes:

However, it is not meant to replace TCP, and it is not meant to be used where TCP has been working well.

The new protocol is particularly designed for distributed data intensive applications where a small number of bulk flows share the abundant optical bandwidth.

The protocol has to be efficient, fair, and friendly. The protocol should be easily deployable and usable. In addition, we want it to be configurable to adapt to more specific situations.
